Lasagna

A chicken taco twist on the classic lasagna.

Here, we will be creating a chicken taco lasagna utilisng various ingredients ranging from 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, to sour cream and lime juice.

Ready? Good.

You will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
  • 1/2 cup of lime juice
  • 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix such as Old EL Paso
  • 1 1/2 cups of cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste

Once you have all of that, you're now ready to make some tastylasagna!
